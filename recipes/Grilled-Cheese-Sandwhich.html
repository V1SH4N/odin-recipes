Description

Grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoon butter,divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Butter one slice of bread
  2. Place bread butter-side down in hot skillet
  3. Add one slice of cheese
  4. Butter a second slice of breas on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese
  5. Cook until cheese is melted
